Swasthya Bot: Intelligent Ayurvedic Health Advisor

Key features

SwasthyaBot, utilising the power of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning algorithms, aims to bridge the gap between the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda and the accessibility needs of the modern world. By systematically processing and analysing the vast repository of Ayurvedic texts, the SwasthyaBot will be able to identify and recommend personalised Ayurvedic remedies based on a user's specific symptoms, dosha constitution, and other relevant factors.

  • Dosha Assessment

    An interactive questionnaire unlocks the secrets of your doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), the fundamental bio-energies that govern your unique constitution.

  • Symptom Analysis

    Tell SwasthyaBot your woes, and its intelligent algorithms will analyse your symptoms to suggest personalised Ayurvedic remedies or treatments

  • Herbal Remedy Suggestions

    Forget harsh chemicals and embrace the gentle power of nature. SwasthyaBot recommends specific herbs and herbal combinations aligned with Ayurvedic principles.

  • Diet and Lifestyle Guidance

    SwasthyaBot guides you towards personalised dietary suggestions and lifestyle modifications.
